## Photos and voice

When you take a photo to show Buddy, we send that photo to our AI provider for analysis. We don't store the photo on our servers. See our [Privacy Policy](./privacy.md) for full details.

When you tap the microphone, the audio stays on your phone. Your phone converts your speech to text, and we send the text to Buddy. We do not record, transmit, or store any audio.

## Scam protection — important to read

**However**: Buddy can be wrong, and Buddy cannot stop a scam from happening. If something on your screen demands urgent action, payment, or asks you to call a phone number — please **stop**, ignore the urgency, and ask Buddy or a trusted family member before doing anything.
